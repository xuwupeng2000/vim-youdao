SUMMARY                                         *vim-youdao*

Easily open the dict.youdao.com page for translating.

                                                *youdao.vim-:youdao*
:Gem [{gem-name}]     Use :youdao rails to open translation page on dict.youdao.com.
                      If the cursor is located on a gem name you can
                      use :youdao to open up the tanslation page for that word.
